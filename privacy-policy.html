Privacy Policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Pedro Almagro Torrecillas ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit and use the Voidlight: Chronicles of the Stellar Veil wiki and character creation tools (the "Service").

Voidlight™: Chronicles of the Stellar Veil is a homebrew tabletop role-playing game created by Pedro Almagro Torrecillas, based on the Daggerheart SRD v1.0. This website provides wiki information, character creation tools, and character management features for players of the game.

By using our Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Character Data: When you use our character creator, we store character information (names, stats, abilities, equipment) locally in your browser using local storage
  • Contact Information: If you contact us directly, we may receive additional information such as your email address and message content

Automatically Collected Information

We use Google Analytics to collect information about how you use our website, including:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, click patterns, and navigation paths
  • Device Information: Browser type, device type, operating system, and screen resolution
  • Technical Data: IP address (anonymized), cookies, and similar tracking technologies
  • Performance Data: Page load times and website performance metrics

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

Essential Functions

  • Providing the character creator and management tools
  • Storing your character data locally in your browser
  • Ensuring the website functions properly

Analytics and Improvement

  • Understanding how users interact with our website
  • Identifying popular content and features
  • Improving website performance and user experience
  • Analyzing usage patterns to enhance our services

Communication

  • Responding to your inquiries and support requests
  • Providing updates about the game system (if you contact us directly)

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • Character data is stored locally in your browser's local storage
  • We do not transmit character data to our servers
  • You can delete this data at any time using the "Clear All Characters" function

Google Analytics

  • Usage data is processed by Google Analytics
  • Google may store this data on servers worldwide
  • We have enabled IP anonymization to protect your privacy
  • Data is retained according to Google's data retention policies

Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

  • Secure transmission of data using HTTPS encryption
  • Regular security assessments of our website
  • Limited data collection to what is necessary for our services

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies:

Essential Cookies

  • Cookie Consent: Remembers your cookie preferences
  • Local Storage: Stores character data locally in your browser

Analytics Cookies (Google Analytics)

  • Performance Monitoring: Tracks how you use our website
  • User Analytics: Helps us understand user behavior and improve our service
  • Session Tracking: Identifies unique visits and user sessions

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through:

  • Your browser settings to block or delete cookies
  • Our cookie consent banner to opt-out of non-essential cookies
  • Google Analytics opt-out tools: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout

Third-Party Services

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how our website is used. Google Analytics:

  • Collects information about your use of our website
  • Uses cookies to identify unique users and sessions
  • Provides us with aggregated, anonymized reports
  • Has its own privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.

  • Right to Access: Request information about the personal data we hold about you

  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request limitation of how we process your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your personal data
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing at any time

All Users

  • Opt-Out of Analytics: Use browser settings or Google's opt-out tools

Data Retention

Character Data

  • Stored locally in your browser until you delete it

  • Persists until you clear browser data or use our "Clear All Characters" function

Analytics Data
