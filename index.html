Overview

In the post-Silence galaxy, humanity struggles to rebuild civilization among shattered star systems. This comprehensive wiki contains all the rules, lore, and mechanics needed to play Voidlight - a game where Hope and Fear shape reality itself.

  • Narrative-first mechanics with Hope and Fear dynamics
  • Rich post-apocalyptic sci-fi setting
  • Comprehensive character customization
  • Political intrigue between Great Houses
  • Advanced technology and equipment systems

Dancing Between Hope and Fear

Duality Dice System

Roll 2d12 - one Hope (bright color), one Fear (dark color). Add together plus modifiers vs Difficulty.

Hope Higher

Gain Hope token, even failures have silver linings

Fear Higher

GM gains Fear token, even successes have complications

Critical Success

Both dice same number - automatic success, gain Hope, clear Stress
